# End-to-End Customer Segmentation Project

## Business Problem
The marketing team wanted to improve campaign targeting by segmenting customers based on behavior, demographics, and purchase history.

## Data
- 30,000 customer profiles
- 2 years of transaction data
- Website engagement metrics

## Tools Used
Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib), SQL, Tableau

## Analysis / Workflow
1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**  
   - Removed duplicates and invalid entries  
   - Normalized numerical features and encoded categorical variables  

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis**  
   - Analyzed spending patterns, frequency, and engagement  
   - Identified high-value and low-value customer groups  

3. **Segmentation Modeling**  
   - K-Means clustering to identify 4 customer segments  
   - Validated clusters with silhouette score  

4. **Visualization**  
   - Created Tableau dashboards to make segments visible to stakeholders  
   - Highlighted top 2 segments for high-priority marketing campaigns  

## Key Insights
- Segment A (10% of customers) contributes 45% of revenue  
- Segment D (25% of customers) shows low engagement but high potential if targeted  
- Seasonal purchase spikes in Segment B indicate opportunity for timely promotions  

## Recommendation
- Launch retention campaigns targeting Segment D  
- Personalize offers for Segment A to maintain loyalty  
- Use Segment B insights to time promotions around seasonal peaks
