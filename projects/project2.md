# Customer Churn Prediction Model

## Business Problem

Customer churn was increasing in mid-tier subscription plans, impacting recurring revenue growth.

---

## Approach

**Data**
- 20,000 customer records
- 12 months behavioral + subscription data

**Methods**
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Feature engineering on engagement metrics
- Logistic Regression + Random Forest modeling
- Model validation with cross-validation and ROC-AUC

---

## Key Findings

- Customers with low engagement in first 3 months were **3x more likely to churn**
- Mid-tier subscription plan showed highest churn rate
- Early inactivity strongly correlated with cancellation risk

---

## Business Impact

- Identified high-risk users before churn
- Enabled targeted retention campaigns
- Provided data-driven recommendations to marketing team
