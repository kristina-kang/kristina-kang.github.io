# Customer Churn Analysis

## Business Problem
The company wanted to reduce customer churn and identify high-risk segments for retention.

## Data
- 20,000 customer records
- 12 months of subscription and usage data

## Tools Used
Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn), SQL, Excel

## Analysis
- Exploratory data analysis on customer activity
- Built logistic regression and random forest models
- Identified features correlated with churn

## Key Insights
- Customers with low engagement in first 3 months were 3x more likely to churn
- High churn rate in mid-tier subscription segment

## Recommendation
Launch early engagement campaigns and targeted offers for high-risk customers.
